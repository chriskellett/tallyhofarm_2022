Camp Rules

Updated 21/09/2020

  • Please pitch your tent a minimum of 6 meters from neighbouring tents or vans.
  • Max speed on camp-site 5mph.
  • If you return after dark please leave your car at the office and walk to your pitch.
  • Please leave all facilities as you would wish to find them.
  • Noise to be kept to a minimum between 10pm & 9am: NO music after 10pm.
  • No climbing, defacing or burning branches of trees allowed.
  • Collecting of wood from the Forest is NOT allowed.
  • Please do not climb over any fences or gates.
  • Trespassing onto neighbouring property STRICTLY forbidden.
  • Campers, children and pets are forbidden in the working farm yard.
  • Fireworks and Chinese Lanterns are STRICTLY prohibited.
  • No chasing of our chickens / Sheep please!
  • Please leave your pitch tidy and litter free on your departure & please help us by sorting your rubbish correctly at the recycling are.
  • NO FLY-TIPPING on the lane or in the woods or neighbouring property.
  • Please drive slowly along the lane back to the main road.
